## Codes:

*Syntax: Race-Initial.Code (Race-InitialRace-Initial.Tr for trade)*

race initials: K,R,C,F

T=tech
A=Ascention
Tr=Trade Agreement
C=Colony
I=succesful invasion of enemy colony
