#Playtest Plan

## Purpose

Evaluate whether Galactic Crisis Mode improves pacing, player interaction and endgame tension compared to vanilla Ascendancy.

## Primary hypotheses

### H1.
Players interact earlier than in vanilla.

### H2.
The alliance phase creates a more dynamic midgame.

### H3.
The war phase occurs after meaningful exploration, but before Ascendancy snowballs.

### H4.
No player is effectively eliminated before the alliance phase.

### H5.
The winner feels earned rather than abrupt.

## Secondary hypotheses

* 40 tension points triggers war at an appropriate time.
* One point per event is sufficient.
* Two points for conquering a colony feels appropriate.
* Fewest resource nodes correctly identifies the weakest player.
* Alliance selection feels fair.
